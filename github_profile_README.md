## 🛰️ What I Build

I sit at the intersection of **geospatial engineering** and **deep learning** — building systems that turn satellite imagery, smartphone sensors, and UAV data into actionable intelligence for infrastructure, environment, and agriculture.

My work is driven by a single question:
